Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?

Absolutely. We are proud to be 100% cruelty-free and all of our skincare and cosmetic products are certified vegan. We never test on animals and use only plant-based ingredients.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and expedited shipping (2-3 business days) within the United States. Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping.

What is your return policy?

We stand by our products with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, please contact us within 30 days for a full refund or exchange.

How can I find the right product for my skin type?

Each product page features detailed information about the ingredients and the skin types it is best suited for. For a personalized recommendation, feel free to reach out to our skincare experts via the contact form!
