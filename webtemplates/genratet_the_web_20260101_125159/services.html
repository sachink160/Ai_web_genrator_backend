Frequently Asked Questions

What is covered under the standard warranty?

The standard warranty typically covers manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions that occur under normal use within the specified warranty period. It generally does not cover accidental damage, liquid damage, or unauthorized repairs. Specific terms vary by product and manufacturer.

How do I check the status of my repair?

You can check the status of your repair by visiting our 'Contact Us' page and using the provided tracking number from your service request. Alternatively, you can call our customer support line with your repair ticket number, and our team will provide an update.

Can I purchase an extended warranty after buying the product?

Yes, in most cases, you can purchase an extended warranty within a certain timeframe (usually 30-90 days) after your initial product purchase. Please contact our sales team or visit our store for more details and available plans.

Do you offer home installation services for large appliances or smart home systems?

For select large appliances and complex smart home systems, we do offer professional home installation services. Please inquire at the time of purchase or contact our service department to check eligibility and schedule an appointment.

What payment methods do you accept for repair services?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and cash for repair services. For certain services, online payment options may also be available. Please confirm with our service center prior to your visit.
