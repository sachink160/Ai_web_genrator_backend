Career services FAQ

When do career services start?
Career support starts in your first week. You’ll complete an onboarding survey, meet your mentor, and attend a career orientation so you understand the milestones ahead.
Is job placement guaranteed?
We don’t promise specific salaries or companies. Instead, we provide a structured, data‑driven process, experienced coaches, and direct access to hiring partners. Your outcomes depend on your engagement and follow‑through, and we’re transparent about that.
How long can I access career support?
Full career services are available for 12 months after your bootcamp end date, and alumni retain access to select resources—like interview refreshers and negotiation workshops—indefinitely.
Do you help with remote or international roles?
Yes. Many of our partners hire remotely across time zones, and we regularly work with students pursuing remote‑first or relocation‑friendly roles. Your coach will help align your search with your location and visa requirements.
Do I need prior tech experience?
No prior professional experience in tech is required. We routinely support teachers, service workers, designers, and professionals from non‑technical backgrounds. Career services are specifically designed with career switchers in mind.
Which bootcamps include career services?
All of our full coding bootcamps include complete career services at no additional cost. Short, standalone courses include access to group workshops and selected career content.

Ready to treat your job search like a team sport?

Tell us where you’re starting from and where you’d like to go. In a 20‑minute consultation, we’ll map out a learning path, a realistic hiring timeline, and how our mentors and coaches can support you along the way.
