Contact & admissions FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about getting in touch, booking calls, and what happens after you reach out.

How quickly will someone respond after I submit the form?
During business hours, most prospective students hear from us within a few hours. Outside of those times, you can expect a response by the next business day. Technical support emails are monitored 24/7.
Is the consultation call really free and no obligation?
Yes. The goal of the call is to understand your goals, background, and constraints. We will help you decide if a coding bootcamp is the right move—and if not, we will recommend alternative paths without any pressure to enroll.
Can I talk directly to an instructor or mentor?
Admissions will be your first point of contact. If a technical deep dive would help, we are happy to arrange a follow‑up conversation with an instructor or mentor so you can see how we teach and support students.
Do you offer in‑person campus visits or only remote meetings?
We are a remote‑first bootcamp and currently do not offer in‑person campus tours. However, we can walk you through our learning platform on a video call and show you how live classes, projects, and mentoring sessions work in practice.
