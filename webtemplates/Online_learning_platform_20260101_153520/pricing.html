Pricing & enrollment FAQs

Answers to the most common questions students ask about tuition, financing, and getting started.

When do I actually have to pay tuition? +

If you’re paying upfront, a deposit is due when you accept your offer, and the remaining balance is due 14 days before your cohort starts. For monthly or deferred tuition plans, you’ll complete a separate agreement with our financing partners; in most cases, payments begin only once your program starts.

Can I keep working while enrolled? +

Yes. Our core bootcamps are designed for working professionals, with live sessions typically held on evenings and weekends. Many students continue working full time and use our flexible schedule and recorded sessions to stay on track without burning out.

Do you offer corporate reimbursement support? +

Absolutely. We can provide itemized invoices, learning outcomes, and completion certificates tailored for your employer’s tuition-assistance process. Our team can also speak directly with your manager or HR team to explain how the program aligns with your current role.

What happens if I need to pause or switch cohorts? +

Life happens. You can request a one-time deferral to a future cohort at no additional tuition cost if circumstances change before or shortly after your program begins. Our student success team will work with you to choose a new start date that fits your schedule.

Is equipment or software included in tuition? +

You’ll need a laptop and a reliable internet connection. We provide access to all required software tools, course platforms, and cloud environments at no additional cost during the program. Optional certifications or exam fees are not included unless explicitly stated.
