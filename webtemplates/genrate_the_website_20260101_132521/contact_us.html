Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, orders, and services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), UPI payments, Net Banking, and popular digital wallets for your convenience. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can use this to monitor your package's journey until it reaches your doorstep.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 7-day return policy for most items. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service within 7 days of delivery to initiate a return or exchange. Personalized items may be subject to different conditions.

Do you offer gift wrapping services?

Yes, we do! You can select our premium gift wrapping option at checkout for a small additional charge. We'll ensure your gift looks beautiful and ready to delight your loved ones.

Can I send a personalized message with my gift?

Absolutely! During the checkout process, you'll have the option to add a personalized gift message. We'll print it on a lovely card and include it with your order, free of charge.
